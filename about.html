Our Story

Crafting Moments of Light

A journey from raw wax to refined fragrance, built on passion and artisanal excellence.

Since 2018

The Heart of Ember & Glow

Founded with a simple mission: to create home fragrances that are as honest and natural as the ingredients we use. What started as a small kitchen experiment has grown into a dedicated artisanal studio.

We believe that a candle is more than just a source of light—it's an invitation to pause, breathe, and reconnect. Every scent in our collection is inspired by real-world memories, from damp pine forests to the quiet warmth of a sun-filled morning.
