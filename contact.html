Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we only ship within the continental US and Canada. We hope to expand our shipping zones very soon!

What is your return policy?

Because of the nature of our products, we accept returns only on unused, unlit candles within 14 days of delivery.

Are your candles vegan?

Yes! All of our products are 100% vegan, cruelty-free, and crafted entirely from natural soy wax without synthetic dyes.

How long do the candles burn?

Our standard 8oz candles burn for approximately 45 hours. Our premium collection burns for up to 80 hours.
