FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I skip a month or pause my subscription?
Absolutely! You can skip, pause, or cancel your subscription at any time through your dashboard. No commitments, no penalties.
Can I choose my own scents each month?
Yes! Before each shipment, we'll send you an email to choose your scents. If you don't pick, we'll surprise you based on your quiz preferences.
When will my subscription box ship?
Subscription boxes ship on the 1st of each month. You'll receive tracking details via email within 24 hours of shipment.
What's included in the workshop benefit?
Enthusiast members get one free Beginner's workshop per quarter. Connoisseur members get unlimited access to all workshop types including private sessions.
Is there a refund policy?
If you're not satisfied with your candles, contact us within 14 days for a full refund or exchange. We want you to love every candle!
